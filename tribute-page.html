About Kishori Amonkar

Kishori Amonkar was an Indian classical vocalist. She is considered to have been one of the foremost singers in the Hindustani tradition and is an innovator of the Jaipur gharana, or a community of musicians sharing a distinctive musical style.

Recognition and legacy

  • Sangeet Natak Akademi Award, 1985

  • Padma Bhushan, 1987

  • Dr. T. M. A. Pai Outstanding Konkani Award, 1991

  • Padma Vibhushan, 2002

  • Sangeet Natak Akademi Fellowship, 2009

  • M.S. Subbulakshmi Award for classical music, 2016
